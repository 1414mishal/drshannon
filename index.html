ENT Insights

Are ENT surgeries painful?

Modern surgical techniques are minimally invasive, significantly reducing pain and recovery time. We prioritize patient comfort at every stage.

How to prevent ear infections?

Avoid using cotton swabs inside the ear canal, keep ears dry after swimming, and manage nasal allergies promptly.

Do you perform laser ENT procedures?

We utilize advanced technologies, including laser and coblation, for specific ear and throat treatments to ensure precision.

What causes frequent sneezing?

This is often due to Allergic Rhinitis. A proper ENT evaluation can help identify triggers and provide long-term relief.
